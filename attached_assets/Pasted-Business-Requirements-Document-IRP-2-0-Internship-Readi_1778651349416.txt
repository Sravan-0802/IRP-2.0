Business Requirements Document
IRP 2.0 Internship Readiness Path: Replit Page 
Document Owner: Student Success Team, NxtWave Academy Prepared For: Product Team Version: 1.0 Date: May 2026 Scope: YOG 2028 & 2029


1. Purpose
This document outlines the requirements for building a dedicated Replit page for the Internship Readiness Path 2.0 (IRP 2.0). The page will serve as the single source of truth for students on the IRP process, levels, eligibility, and timelines — and will integrate the Accounts Form for assessment registration.


2. Background & Context
IRP 2.0 is a redesigned internship readiness program built exclusively for YOG 2028 and 2029 students. Unlike the previous IRP (which had offline rounds and common assessments with senior batches), IRP 2.0 is:

100% online
Year-level tailored (assessments scoped to what 1st and 2nd year students have studied)
Multi-level (Level 1 → Level 2 → Level 3 internship opportunities)

Once a student clears both Level 1 and Level 2, they become eligible for internship opportunities (5K–10K stipend range). Students aiming for 25K+ stipend internships must additionally qualify for Level 3 — the Infinite Aura track — which is a curated path for top performers who clear both levels. Both pathways and their eligibility criteria must be clearly communicated on the page.
